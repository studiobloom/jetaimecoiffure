Xhanan Villa (Xhana)

JE T’AIME COIFFURE SALON OWNER/ HAIR DESIGNER/ L'OREAL PROFESSIONNEL ARTIST/ EDUCATOR

The world of beauty has and will continue to light a fire in Xhana’s heart. Xhana has been lucky enough to travel around and out of the country to further her education in the beauty industry along with furthering the education of stylists like herself who have the motivation and drive to make people love their hair. As a young adult living in a country that was in the midst of a war all while pursuing a medical career for the safety of herself and her husband, Xhana was forced to abandon her dream of becoming a doctor and relocate to the United States. She and her husband made their way to America with no promised future where she decided to enroll in beauty school. She found hope and a new dream to pursue. That day she made a promise to herself that if this was the path she was choosing, she was going to do everything in her power to continually further her knowledge and enhance her skills to be the best hair expert she could be. Fast forward 22 years later and she continues pushing herself to be skilled in this industry. From being a stylist assistant in New York City to being the cofounder of Je T’aime Coffuire and becoming a part of the L’Oreal Professionnel family, nothing is more satisfying for Xhana then pursuing her dream.

MaryLou S.

HAIR DESIGNER

Marylou first began her career in the industry by attending the Wilfred Academy and has continued her education throughout the years. Her vast training includes studying under John and Suzanne Chadwick of NY, as well as at the Jingles Hairdressing Academy. She later trained with Beth Minardi in all aspects of hair color at her NYC salon, as well as with Paul Mitchell, also in NYC. Mary Lou’s experience eventually led her to becoming co-owner of a Barber and Unisex Haircutting Salon for 7 years, and later to be part-owner of Ariston Hair Design, located in RedBank, for 17 years. She has attended advanced classes at The Sassoon Academy (San Francisco), The Aveda Institute for Haircolor(NYC), Goldwell Master classes (NYC) and is certified in Japanese hair straightening and Goldwell Keratin Relaxers. Mary Lou embraces city trends and change with a creative eye and an objective ear to bring your best look into focus.
