About Us

We believe you deserve nothing less than an affordable luxury to leave you refreshed, uplifted, and feeling heavenly. If you believe experiencing something pleasurable is essential, then visit Je T’aime Coiffure at Galleria, located in a former turn-of-the-century uniform factory. Our urbanized and distinctive hair salon offers you the highest standard of hairdressing alongside exceptional customer service all in an updated sophisticated atmosphere. We offer a full line of products to cater to the individual customer’s needs. Our beauty treatments include styling, cutting, coloring, highlighting, keratin treatments, relaxers, basic perms, hair extensions, makeup, waxing, and more.
